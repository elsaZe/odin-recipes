Baby Dal

Description

This sweet potato dal is perfect for babies who are transitioning to solids. It is slightly sweet and slightly spicy. Your baby will be goo-ing and gah-ing for more!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 3 Sweet Potatoes
  • 1 cup of lentils
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp curry
  • 4 cups of water

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a medium sized sauce pan.
  2. Dice onion and add to the oil.
  3. Cook onion until translucent. This should take approximately 5 minutes
  4. Peal and cut sweat potatos into 1-inch cubes
  5. Once onions are cooked add cumin and curry to pot. Cook the spices in the oil for about 1 minute until fragrent.
  6. Add the sweet potatos, lentils and water. Cook until sweet potatos are soft.
  7. When the dal is done cooking, gently mash the sweet potatoes and lentils together.
  8. Serve warm.
