Blueberry Beets and Bananas

Description

This puree is a delightful combination of foods. You may even consider eating it as an adult summer soup!

Ingredients

  • 4 medium sized beets
  • 1 pint of blueberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tsp of rosemary

Direction

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Wrap the beets in tin foil and place in preheated oven for about 45 minutes until soft.
  3. When beets have cooled, gentlyl rub the skin to remove.
  4. Place peeled beets, peeled banana blueberries and rosemary into a blender
  5. Blend until smooth
  6. Serve cold or room temperature
